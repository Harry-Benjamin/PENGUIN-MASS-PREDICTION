AI TO PREDICT A PENGUIN BODY MASS

This AI model predicts the body mass of a penguin based on its physical characteristics such as culmen length and depth, flipper length, species, sex and location.

It is designed to assist researchers and wildlife enthusiasts in weighing penguins without heavy lifting.
